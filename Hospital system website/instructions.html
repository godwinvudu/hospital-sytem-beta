How to Use the Hospital System

  1. Sign Up: Create your account on the Signup page. Fill in your name, age, NHIS number, region, city, and password.
  2. Login: If you already have an account, use the Login page to access the system.
  3. Select Region: Choose your region (Accra, Eastern, or Ashanti) to view hospitals available in that region.
  4. Select Hospital: Click on the hospital image to select it. This will take you to the Services page.
  5. View Services: On the Services page, see the list of available hospital services. Confirm your selection.
  6. Get WAP Code: After confirming the hospital, the system will generate your unique WAP code to access the hospital network.
  7. Access Hospital: Use the WAP code at the hospital to connect to their system and be added to the service queue.
Note: Your account and WAP code are stored temporarily in your browser. Do not clear browser storage while using the system.
